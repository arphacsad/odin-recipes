White Chocolate-Orange-Pistachio Thumbprint Cookies

Nutty pistachio cookies filled with creamy, citrusy white chocolate are an impressive and tasty little treat!

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Additional Time: 1 hr

Total Time: 1 hrs 40 mins

Servings: 36

Yield: 36 cookies

Ingredients

Cookies:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix

Filling:

  • 2 cups white chocolate chips
  • ¼ cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 2 ounces roasted, salted pistachios, finely chopped

Steps

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Combine butter, sugar, egg yolks, and vanilla extract in large bowl. Beat using an electric mixer until well mixed.
